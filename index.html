The Challenge

What we're actually looking for

We're not looking for people who can just solve LeetCode problems. Everyone can do that now.

We're looking for the next generation of leaders. People who take ownership, solve real problems, and have the attitude that companies actually want to hire.

We don't look at your resume. Your college name doesn't matter here. What you present in the challenge IS your resume.

That's why this challenge is weighted 70% behavioral, 30% DSA.

70%

Behavioral

You'll be given real-world scenarios and asked to demonstrate how you think, lead, and solve problems.

You'll also document real situations from your own life where you've demonstrated leadership principles. Ownership, bias for action, learning from failure, delivering results.

These will be background checked with your references. Don't make things up.

30%

DSA

Timed coding problems testing data structures and algorithms fundamentals.

Auto-graded with test cases. Standard interview-level difficulty.

Duration: 3 hours
Evaluation: Behavioral reviewed using Amazon's Leadership Principles framework. DSA auto-graded.
References required: Your real-life examples will be verified.
